Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information regarding Dry Oar's services, activities, and pricing. Customers will often pass on a company or service for other competitors if they do not have a website because customers prefer a simple and independant way to learn about your company without having to wait on the phone. This will lead to higher customer acquisition, resulting in increased revenue.

Audience

The site should be focused towards family oriented adults, particularly parents with pre-teen to teenage children. It should provide them the assurance that the activities look fun and safe for everyone. They will be an outdoors loving family, potentially looking for summer programs and camps for their children, or day activities for the whole family. The colors will need to have some vibrancy, but focus on water and earthy palettes. Both mobiles and desktops will be commonly used. Desktop will be mostly focused on when parents are planning large trips or summer programs. Mobile will be more common for looking up information on the go or looking for a day use.
